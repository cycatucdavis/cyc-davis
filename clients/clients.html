Frequently Asked Questions

What is CYC's project structure?

We undertake two projects for both the fall/winter and spring quarters, each engagement lasting from 10 to 12 weeks. Projects in the fall/winter quarter generally run from mid-November to early February, while projects in the spring quarter go from late February to late May.

In order to ensure the best service possible, each project is staffed with 1 Engagement Manager, 3-4 Consultants, and 1 Faculty advisor.

How are interactions between our clients and team conducted?

The client and the Engagement Manager communicate on a weekly basis to ensure the project's progress. Meetings occur throughout the project engagement, beginning with at least one mandatory project scoping meeting with the Engagement Manager and vice president of engagement at the onset.

What resources does CYC have to ensure success?

The CYC chapter at UC Davis strongly relies upon the expertise of more experienced Engagement Managers and faculty advisors to provide the best quality service.

In addition, our consultants and engagement managers are trained weekly by a cohort of industry professionals of leading consulting firms, Graduate School of Management advisors, and nonprofit leaders.
